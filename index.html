Cherry Tomato

Prolific fruiter — needs full sun and consistent watering.

Basil

Aromatic culinary herb — pinch regularly for bushiness.

Pothos

Low-maintenance trailing vine — thrives in low light.

Snake Plant

Hardiest houseplant — water sparingly, air purifier.

Fast grower with baby plantlets — easy to propagate.
