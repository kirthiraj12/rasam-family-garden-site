Care guide

Sun

Full sun is best.

Water

Water sparingly and allow the soil to dry between waterings.

Soil

Sandy, fast-draining soil.

Fertilizer

Feed very lightly or skip if planted in rich soil.

Pruning

Remove spent flowers to encourage more bloom.

Companions

Pairs nicely with other sun-loving annuals.
